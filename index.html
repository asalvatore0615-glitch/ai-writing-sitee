What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI tool that helps students ask questions, explore ideas, and refine writing. It acts like an intelligent writing partner that supports brainstorming, research, explanations, revision, and academic organization.

Research Findings

Research from 2024–2025 shows:

  • 50%+ of college students use AI for writing.
  • AI boosts understanding of academic materials by ~70%.
  • Students complete work faster with clearer writing.

Benefits of ChatGPT

  • Helps with research and topic exploration
  • Brainstorms ideas and essay structures
  • Improves grammar and clarity
  • Supports vocabulary and phrasing

Risks & Limitations

  • AI can be inaccurate or outdated
  • Responses may include bias
  • Can cause over-dependence
  • Not all conversations are private

Ethical Use

  • Always include AI disclosure when required
  • Do not plagiarize from AI text
  • Keep your own voice and ideas
  • Use AI as support—not a replacement
